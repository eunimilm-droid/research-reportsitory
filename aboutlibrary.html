Library Overview

URSM CAMPUS LIBRARY

The URSM Campus Library serves as the academic knowledge hub of the University, supporting instruction, research, and extension through accessible and student-centered services.

It plays an essential role in helping students, faculty, and staff meet their informational and educational needs by providing organized resources, guided assistance, and a learning-friendly environment.

As a center for knowledge and discovery, the library promotes responsible use of information, independent study, and continuous academic growth in support of the University's goals and philosophy.

“A library is more than a place for books — it is a place where learning, research, and service come together.”

Library Principles

Vision, Mission and Core Values

Library Vision

A technological resource center possessing up-to-date and quality information sources that caters to the development for a lifelong learning in the academic and technical activities of the University.

Library Mission

The library promotes and maintains quality service which supports the University’s philosophy and objectives as well as the instructional, research and extension programs through an organized, relevant, effective and efficient delivery of information in pursuit of quality collection, facilities and services to attain global acceptance and recognition.

Library Core Values

  • Serve the academic community of URS promptly, efficiently and with outmost courtesy.
  • Extend qaulity service to everybody at all times and at all means.
  • Render extra effort in acquiring various learning resource materials.
  • Value books and other library materials as sources of knowledge; and
  • Empower education through research and academic outputs
