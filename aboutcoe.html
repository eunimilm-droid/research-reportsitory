College of Education Overview

COLLEGE OF EDUCATION

The College of Education is committed to producing competent, values-driven, and globally competitive educators.

It provides quality instruction, research opportunities, and extension services that equip students with the necessary knowledge, skills, and attitudes for effective teaching.

The College fosters excellence, leadership, and lifelong learning among future educators.

“Education is the foundation of progress, and teachers are the builders of the future.”

Vision, Mission and Goal

Vision

The College of Education shall be a center of excellence for the development of the world-class educational leaders.

Mission

The College of Education aims to develop highly competent and committed mentors supportive of national and global trust.

Goal

The College of Education aims to improve professional and competent teachers with knowledge, skills, and desirable attitudes and values.
